In a developing nation like India, why Can't One in Ten Girls Afford Sanitary Products?

By placing sanitary napkin dispensers in public places and making it accessible at any time of the day , the initiative hashtag code red concentrates on these major challenges. The main goal of Hashtag code red is to provide free menstruation supplies to women everywhere, including in remote places.

The sanitary napkin dispensers are built to meet established market quality standards and can be installed inside the restroom or anywhere else that's necessary. Many of the girls are afraid to deal with their menstrual cycles since they are unaware of its occurrence; it is our top priority to provide them with hygienic sanitation facilities.
